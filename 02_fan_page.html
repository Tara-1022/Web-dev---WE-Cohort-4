Who is she?


Born Julia Elizabeth Wells, Julie Andrews is a beloved actress, singer and author. Starting her career out in Broadway, she was dubbed 'Britain's youngest prima donna' and quickly rose to prominence in multiple mucicals. Her feature film debut was as Mary Poppins in Walt Disney's children's movie...... well, Mary Poppins (we had a DVD of this movie and as a child I was obsessed with it. Also in love with her because she's practically perfect in every way.)


She's also well known for her role as Maria Von Trapp in The Sound of Music, a beloved musical film classic (We also had a DVD of this one!). The role won her the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress!


Speaking of roles, she also played as the Queen in The Princess Diaries series, and lent her voice to Shrek and Despicable Me franchises.
